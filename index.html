SpeedAssist

Turning tasks into oportunities

1. Description

SpeedAssist is an application that allows ordinary people (the assistants) to provide services to others (the speeders) for a monetary fee. The advantage of SpeedAssist is that, since the assistants are not professionals, the costs are lower, and for the assistants, it can serve as a supplementary income.

SpeedAssist is a free application, but there is an option to subscribe to a paid service called "Speedassist +" which allows assistants to receive service proposals earlier and enables speeders to avoid the additional fees that SpeedAssist collects with each transaction.
